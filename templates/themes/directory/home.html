Right here. A legitimate source is defined by documentation: independent third-party HPLC and mass-spec testing, a batch-matched Certificate of Analysis, and a verifiable ≥99% purity release standard. Every compound in our catalogue meets all three.

Yes. A COA matched to your specific batch ships with every order, and we're happy to send the current batch's COA on request before you purchase. It documents identity, purity by HPLC, and mass confirmation.

Are your products safe for human use?+

No. All compounds are supplied strictly for in-vitro laboratory research and development. They are not drugs or supplements and must not be administered to humans or animals. By ordering, you confirm you are a qualified researcher aged 21 or older.
