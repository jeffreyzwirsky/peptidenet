What "purity" actually means

When we say ≥99%, we mean each released batch is tested and must show 99% or more of the target compound by HPLC area — with mass spec confirming it's the right molecule. Impurities are the leftover 1% or less.

It's the single most useful number on a COA. A supplier that shares it — with the raw chromatogram behind it — is a supplier taking the science seriously.

It means these compounds are sold strictly for laboratory and in-vitro research by qualified professionals. They are not drugs, supplements, or food, and are not intended for human or veterinary use.
